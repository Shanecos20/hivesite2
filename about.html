Our Journey

The HIVE Story

How a simple idea evolved into a revolutionary beekeeping solution that's changing the industry.

A Vision Born from Necessity

HIVE began in 2020 when our founder, a third-generation beekeeper, witnessed firsthand the devastating effects of colony collapse disorder. Frustrated by the lack of advanced tools to monitor and protect his hives, he envisioned a technology that could bridge the gap between traditional beekeeping practices and modern innovation.

Collaborating with engineers, data scientists, and fellow beekeepers, we developed a system that harnesses the power of IoT and AI to provide unprecedented insights into hive health and behavior. Our early prototypes quickly gained attention for their effectiveness in preventing hive loss and increasing honey production.

Today, HIVE has grown into a comprehensive platform used by thousands of beekeepers across Europe and North America, from hobbyists with a single hive to commercial operations with thousands of colonies.

Innovation

Our Technology

The revolutionary system that's transforming beekeeping through smart technology and data-driven insights.

IoT Sensor Network

Our proprietary network of sensors is designed specifically for the unique environment of beehives. These low-power, high-precision devices continuously monitor crucial metrics without disturbing the colony.

Temperature and humidity sensors accurate to within 0.1°C/1% RH

Weight monitoring system with precision load cells

Acoustic monitoring for bee activity and queen status

AI-Powered Analytics

Our machine learning algorithms analyze billions of data points to identify patterns and predict potential issues before they become problems. This predictive approach enables proactive management rather than reactive intervention.

98% accuracy in predicting swarming events 5-7 days in advance

Early detection of disease markers and parasite presence

Personalized recommendations based on local conditions and hive history

Mobile & Web Platform

Our intuitive, cross-platform application puts powerful tools in the hands of beekeepers everywhere. From comprehensive data visualization to community insights, everything you need is just a tap away.

Real-time alerts and notifications when attention is needed

Comprehensive record-keeping and reportng tools

Secure cloud storage with offline capabilities for remote locations
