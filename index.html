How HIVE Works

Our innovative system makes advanced beekeeping technology accessible to everyone from hobbyists to commercial apiaries.

1

Install IoT Sensors

Our modular sensors seamlessly integrate with any hive type. Simply place them in strategic locations to collect comprehensive data about your colony's environment and activity.

Download our intuitive app and pair it with your sensors. The setup wizard guides you through the process in minutes, with no technical expertise required.
